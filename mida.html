Mida Creek

Did you Know?

Interesting & Useful facts
1 Recognized as an Important Bird Area (IBA) where migratory birds from Europe and Eurasia find a place to rest as they migrate.
2 It comprises of different types of habitats that are influenced by the tide, for example, mudflats, sand flats. Open shallow waters and mangrove forests.
3 Mida Creek is a recognized International Bird Area and together with Arabuko-Sokoke Forest forms a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve.
4 Mida Creek is one of the most productive mangrove ecosystems in the world.

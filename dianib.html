Diani Beach

Did you Know?

Interesting & Useful facts
1 Diani is the most popular and beautiful beach in Kenya.
2 It has been voted Africa's leading beach destination for the third time running since 2015.
3 This long stretch of beach is located on Indian Ocean coast, 30 Km south of Mombasa.
4 Diani is renowned for its coral reefs, underwater sandbars, and widespread palm vegetation.
5 The beach is a delightful location for swimming, surfing, snorkeling, sunbathing on the beach, diving, kite surfing among others.

Tour Activities and Accommodation
Itinerary - 2Nights 3Days
Day 1 offers two variants of schedule
We will pick your party in Nairobi and drive across Kenya to our final destination Masai Mara, upon arrival there will be check-in at the Tented Camps for your accommodation and lunch (around 3pm) before you make your selection.
Option 1: Enter the game reserve and spend time observing the wildlife a few hours before dusk.
Option 2: Visit the Maasai Tribe and learn their culture and history while walking in their village and seeing their proud heritage.
Day 2. After breakfast we embark on our full day journey into the Masai Mara Game Reserve. Lunch will be provided inside the park. As the sun sets you will be returned to your accomodations where you may relax and enjoy our dinner meal.
Day 3. You checkout, and we prepare our return to Nairobi after your breakfast. Before leaving you will be given the option of visiting the Maasai Tribe Village for a last chance of getting some authentic souvenirs of your visit to Masai Mara.
